Our Story

Baked with Passion in Ottawa

Welcome to Madame La Joie! We are a home-based artisan bakery in Ottawa, specializing in custom cakes, cupcakes, and pastries for every occasion.

Every creation is baked from scratch using premium ingredients and decorated with meticulous attention to detail. Whether it's a birthday, wedding, baby shower, or just a sweet craving — we bring joy to your celebrations.

"La joie" means "joy" in French, and that's exactly what we put into every single cake.
